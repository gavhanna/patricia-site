TRUE NORTH

The fifth in the series Marking the Land true north explores the stories we are told that make up our cultural history. Part of these stories was the ancient Celtic sites of Zavist in the Czech Republic, Hallstatt in Austria and La Tene in Switzerland.

Interventions were made in these sites where objects were floated on lakes made out of ephemeral and dissolvable material. A video was made of the overland journey from Dublin to Austria and Switzerland. The installation was shown in the West Cork Arts centre.
