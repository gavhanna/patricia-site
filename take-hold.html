TAKE HOLD

This show in the Bluewall Gallery County Cavan looked at how we are shaped by place and create our own Landscapes both internal and external. The gallery space contained what appeared to be a tree hanging upside down but in fact it consisted of a number of different materials including drawings, steel, thread and branches from a number of different sources including willow. A short video and a series of drawings and small sculptures completed the installation.
