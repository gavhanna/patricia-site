JOURNEY

Journey is a series of pieces exploring the forces and movements that shape us and help us understand our personal and cultural identity.

This is a continuation of the work done during the Soil project. "Journey", continuing from "Soil", questions the forces and movements that shape us and help us understand our personal and cultural identity.

The body of work "Journey" began in the Rockefeller Center in Bellagio in Italy when labelled soil from Ireland was placed there.
