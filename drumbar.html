DRUMBAR

Drumbar is the second in the series Marking the Land.

Unlike the other projects this was held first in Temple Bar Gallery, Dublin and later in a national school in Blacklion County Cavan. Drumbar is the townland name of my father's home. The Installation consisted of a large free standing form and Paintings and drawings.

This work explored further issues of displacement, emigration and family roots.
